Food in Goa

Non Vegitarian Food option

Goa is famous for its sea food specially rice and delicious fish curry along with coconuts. Goa also has a famous chicken dish called the chicken xacuti. Goa also has many other non vegitarian foods that include its egg curry, chicken vindaloo, pork scorpotel, chicken cutlet, mutton vindaloo, chorizo pulao, and ros omlette.

Vegitarian Food option

There is also yummy food provided for the vegitarians that include a coin sized puffed bread wrapped  around curried baby potatoes, vegetable chop, khatkhatem, ambadyachem sasav, breadfruit phodi, chidki midki bhagi, dal, and mooga gathi.
